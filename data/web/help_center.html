Help Center

Account Support

Users can verify email, reset passwords, manage security settings, and control active sessions from their account settings.

Billing Support

Billing questions may include upgrades, downgrades, failed payments, and subscription cancellation.

Document Support

If a document fails to upload or becomes unsearchable, users should check processing status and confirm that the file contains extractable text.

Answer Quality

If answers are incomplete or weak, users should review document quality, remove duplicates, and ask more specific questions.

When to Contact Support

Contact support if account access cannot be restored, billing issues continue, or indexing errors remain unresolved after retrying standard troubleshooting steps.
