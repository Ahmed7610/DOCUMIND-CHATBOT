Pricing Plans

Free

The Free plan is designed for light usage and product evaluation.

  • Up to 5 AI queries per day
  • Up to 3 uploaded documents
  • Basic search and chat interface

Pro

The Pro plan is intended for professionals and small teams that need higher limits and better support.

  • Unlimited AI queries
  • Up to 100 uploaded documents
  • Analytics dashboard access
  • Priority support

Price: $19/month

Enterprise

The Enterprise plan provides organizational controls and integration features.

  • Unlimited documents
  • API access
  • Advanced admin controls
  • Dedicated support

Price: Custom pricing

Free Trial
